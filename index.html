East Tennessee Cuisine and Food Culture

East Tennessee's food culture is deeply rooted in Appalachian traditions while embracing contemporary culinary trends. The region is famous for Tennessee-style BBQ, Southern comfort food, and modern Appalachian cuisine that reimagines traditional mountain recipes with contemporary techniques.

From Knoxville's downtown food scene to the tourist destinations of Gatlinburg and Pigeon Forge, and the riverside restaurants of Chattanooga, East Tennessee offers incredible diversity. Local ingredients include Tennessee whiskey, Benton's bacon from Madisonville, Cruze Farm dairy products, and seasonal produce from area farms.
